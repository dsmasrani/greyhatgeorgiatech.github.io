GreyHat

About

GreyHat is Georgia Tech's Cybersecurity club. We focus on teaching practical skills in analyzing, exploiting, and defending computer systems, along with reverse engineering of applications. In addition, we have a competitive Capture-the-Flag (CTF) team. All skill levels are welcome, even if you've never heard the word "cybersecurity" before!

GreyHat holds two weekly meetings at CCB 0017 (basement).

- Tuesdays: CTF team meetings where we will solve CTF problems (What is a ctf?).

- Thursdays: general meeting in which members or guest lecturers present on cyber security topics

Past presentations can be found in our archives!
