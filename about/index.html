GreyHat

about

If you’ve ever been interested in information security but don’t know where to begin, GreyHat is the perfect place to get started! We are the Georgia Tech cyber security club.

GreyHat holds two weekly meetings: a general meeting in which members or guest lecturers present on cyber security topics and a ctf meeting for ctf practice and study (What is a ctf?).

We usually meet on Mondays and Thursdays at 6:30pm in CCB 017.

Past presentations can be found in our archives!
