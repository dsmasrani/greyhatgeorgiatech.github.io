About

GreyHat is Georgia Tech's Cybersecurity club. We focus on teaching practical skills in analyzing, exploiting, and defending computer systems, along with reverse engineering of applications. In addition, we have a competitive Capture-the-Flag (CTF) team. All skill levels are welcome, even if you've never heard the word "cybersecurity" before!

GreyHat holds two weekly meetings:.

- Tuesdays (6:30PM in Klaus 1447): CTF team meetings where we will solve CTF problems (What is a ctf?).

- Thursdays (6:30PM in Howey L3): general meeting in which members or guest lecturers present on cyber security topics

Presentations from prior years can be found in the #archives channel on Discord or in our archives!

Join our Discord group to get updates about our meetings and CTFs!
