GreyHat

About

If you’ve ever been interested in information security but don’t know where to begin, GreyHat is the perfect place to get started! We are the Georgia Tech cyber security club. We do CTF's, promote security on campus, and much more!

GreyHat holds two weekly meetings at 6:30 PM EST on BlueJeans: BlueJeans Link

- Mondays: CTF team meetings where we will solve CTF problems (What is a ctf?).

- Thursdays: general meeting in which members or guest lecturers present on cyber security topics

Past presentations can be found in our archives!
